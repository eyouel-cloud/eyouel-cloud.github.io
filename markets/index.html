How The TikTok Flow Works

Step 1

Creator authorization

The creator signs in with TikTok and grants the exact scopes required for profile identification and Direct Post.

Step 2

Creator info check

The backend queries TikTok creator settings so the app displays available privacy and interaction controls.

Step 3

Direct Post consent

The creator reviews the video, caption, privacy, comments, duet, stitch, disclosure, and AI label before posting.

Content Boundary

Allowed

Financial education

Educational explainers about equities, ETFs, public company earnings, market indexes, economic calendars, and portfolio risk concepts.

Focused

Stock market only
