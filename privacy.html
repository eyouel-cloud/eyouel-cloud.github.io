Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

EyouelExchange ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

EyouelExchange does not collect personal information from website visitors or social media followers. The application itself only accesses data belonging to its own operator-owned accounts via authorized API connections (e.g., Instagram Graph API, YouTube Data API, TikTok Content Posting API), such as basic profile information (user ID, display name, avatar) and content publishing permissions.

2. How We Use Information

Account-level API access (e.g., user.info.basic, video.upload) is used solely to automate publishing of content created by the operator to the operator's own accounts. No data is shared, sold, or transferred to any third party.

3. Data Storage

API credentials and tokens are stored securely and used only by the automation system operated by the account owner. No end-user data from third parties is stored.

4. Third-Party Services

This tool integrates with third-party platforms (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, X) solely to publish content. Each platform's own privacy policy governs data handled by that platform.

5. Children's Privacy

This tool and its associated accounts are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the associated accounts and integrations constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the account owner via the contact information listed on the linked social media profiles.
