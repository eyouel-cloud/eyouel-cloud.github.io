Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Eyouel Exchange ("we," "our," or "the service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when authorized creators use our website and TikTok publishing workflow.

1. Information We Collect

When a creator signs in with TikTok, TikTok may provide an authorization code that our server exchanges for access credentials. Depending on the permissions granted, we may process the creator's TikTok open ID, display name, avatar, posting permissions, token expiration details, selected video metadata, publishing status, and related error/status responses returned by TikTok.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information only to authenticate the creator, retrieve current creator posting settings, prepare original Eyouel Exchange crypto market videos for review, and publish creator-approved content through TikTok's Content Posting API. We do not sell personal information.

3. Data Storage And Security

OAuth tokens and API credentials are stored in the server-side publishing system and are not embedded in this public website. We apply reasonable technical safeguards to protect access tokens, refresh tokens, and client secrets.

4. User Control

Creators control whether to authorize the TikTok connection and whether to publish each post. A creator may revoke app access from TikTok account settings at any time. Requests to delete stored authorization data can be sent using the contact method listed below.

5. Third-Party Services

The service integrates with TikTok for login and content posting. TikTok's own terms and privacy policy apply to data handled by TikTok. The Eyouel Exchange website also links to social media profiles that are governed by their respective platform policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will remain available on this page.

8. Contact

Questions or deletion requests can be sent through the public Eyouel Exchange social profile listed on the homepage.
